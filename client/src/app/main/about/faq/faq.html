 What is this app?

This app was created for GT OMSCS students to get a feel for course difficulty and workload.

It is not endorsed or sponsered by the official OMSCS program or staff.

 Does this sync with the old Google doc?

No. There was a one-time import of that data on November 5, 2015.

While the Google doc is still accessible, its use is discouraged as it lacks validation and other useful features.

 I am concerned about privacy. How is my information kept secure?

The app records only your name, email, profile image URL, and OMSCS specialization (if set).

Firebase's security infrastructure guarantees that this information is accessible only to individuals who are registered and authenticated.

See for yourself here.

 How do I publish a review?

After signing in, use the floating action button for composing a review that appears on the reviews page for the given course.

 How can I deep link to a particular review?

Click on the review's time to copy a deep link to the particular review.

 How do I remain anonymous?

Go to your Profile and enable the Remain anonymous to others? switch. This causes your profile image and name to be masked.

 How do I enter grades?

Grades for the OMSCS sections are imported from Georgia Tech's self service reports website, so you don't have to do anything.

Please note that there can be delays in the import process due to various reasons.

 What is the caching policy?

The app caches all data to local storage (if possible).

The cache expires after 24 hours.

This conservative caching policy was implemented as a result of intense bandwidth consumption ($$$).

You can clear your browser's cache if you need the data sooner.

 Need to report an issue or an enhancement request?
